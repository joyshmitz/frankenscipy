VERDICT: baseline lowers Simd<f64,8> to 8x 128-bit XMM ops; +avx2 lowers it to 4x 256-bit YMM ops
         (2 vmulpd + 2 vaddpd per 8-lane chunk). +fma adds NO vfmadd => rustc keeps
         fp-contract=off => the AVX2 widening is BIT-IDENTICAL (same ops, same rounding order).
         fsci ships the baseline column on a fleet whose CPUs report cpu:+avx2 +fma.
